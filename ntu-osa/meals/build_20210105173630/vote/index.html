2020 最佳餐廳投票活動

活動宗旨

為瞭解本校教職員工及學生對於校內餐飲業者所提供服務品質之評價，並對於提供優良服務餐飲廠商予以鼓勵，特辦理本活動，由全校教職員工及學生參與網路投票，以共同票選出各項目優良服務餐飲廠商。

In order to understand the evaluation of the school's faculty, staff and students on the quality of the services provided by the catering industry on campus, and to encourage catering vendors to provide excellent services, this activity was specially organized. The entire school’s faculty, staff and students participated in online voting and voted for each project. Excellent service catering vendors.

主辦單位

本校學生會、膳食協調委員會執行小組

Executive Team of the Student Union and Food Coordination Committee

投票資格

本次投票資格為本校學生及現職教職員工(含約聘人員.計畫人員..等)

Eligibility for this vote is the school's students and current faculty and staff (including contractors, planners, etc.)

投票方式

請以學校計中帳號及密碼登錄投票系統

Please log in to the voting system with the school account and password

票選期間

109 年 11 月 4 日至 11 月 17 日

May 4 to May 17, 109

iPad Air、Airpods、NTU 短袖T、藍牙喇叭、Outdoor 後背包、高級餐廳抵用券、禮物卡…等獎品

iPad Air, Airpods, NTU short sleeve T, Bluetooth speakers, Outdoor backpack, high-end restaurant voucher, gift card... and other prizes

票選結果現場公告及抽獎日期

票選結果現場公告及抽獎日期 109 年 6 月 4 日(星期四) 中午 12 時 20 分

※備註:票選結果及中獎名單將於6月8日(星期一) 公佈於膳食協調委員會網站

On-site announcement of voting results and lucky draw date: June 4, 109 (Thursday) 12:20 noon

※Remarks: The voting results and the winning list will be announced on the website of the Food Coordination Committee on June 8 (Monday)
